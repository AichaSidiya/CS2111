Mademoiselle Coroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Coroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres and today hanged in the Louvre. It is the third of the three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Media: D1 on Canon
Dimension: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louvre Paris
